## Method

### Lentil curry

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot. Sauté the onion until soft.

2. Add the garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.

3. Stir in the curry powder, paprika and turmeric.

4. Add the lentils, chickpeas, coconut milk and stock. Stir until well combined. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 20–25 minutes until the lentils are soft.

5. Stir through the spinach at the end until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.

### Crispy tofu

1. Press the tofu for 5 minutes using paper towel. Cut into cubes or slices, season with salt and pepper.

2. Pan fry in olive oil over medium heat for 3–4 minutes per side until golden.

## Serving suggestions

Serve with wholemeal sourdough, crispy tofu and fresh parsley on top.
