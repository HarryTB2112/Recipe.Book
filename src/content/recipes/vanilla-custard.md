## Method

1. Whisk the egg yolks, sugar and cornflour together in a bowl.

2. Warm the milk in a saucepan until steaming but not boiling, then slowly whisk it into the egg mixture.

3. Pour it back into the pan and cook over low heat, whisking constantly, until thickened to your liking - about 5–6 minutes.

4. Stir in the vanilla.
