## Method

1. Season the potatoes and air fry at 180°C for 20–25 minutes until crispy.

2. Get a large pot on medium-high heat, add oil and the diced onion. Cook until slightly browned.

3. Add the pork mince and cook until fully browned.

4. Add the mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes.

5. Add the chopped cabbage and cook for 3 minutes.

6. Add the gochujang, oyster sauce, honey and sesame oil. Mix really well.

7. Serve on the potatoes and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
