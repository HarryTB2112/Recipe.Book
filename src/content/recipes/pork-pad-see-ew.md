## Method

1. Boil the rice noodles according to packet instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. Brown the pork mince in a large wok over high heat.

3. Add the onion, spring onions and garlic, cook until softened.

4. Add the Chinese broccoli, chilli and bean sprouts. Stir fry until the broccoli wilts.

5. Push everything to the side of the wok and scramble the eggs.

6. Add the noodles and sauce, toss thoroughly until everything is well coated.

7. Garnish with sesame seeds.
