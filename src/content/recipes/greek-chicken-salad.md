## Method

1. Poach the chicken legs in the stock for 25 minutes.

2. Once cooked, remove and allow to cool completely. Shred the chicken with your fingers into a bowl.

3. Add the green onion, celery, tzatziki, chickpeas, mustard, onion powder, garlic powder and lime juice. Mix well.

4. Serve with pitta bread.
