## Method

### Ragu

1. In a large pan, drizzle a glug of olive oil and place on medium heat.

2. Add the onion, garlic, carrot and celery. Season with salt and pepper and cook until the onion has softened.

3. Add the mince and cook for 10 minutes until browned.

4. Add the tomato purée and hot honey gochujang paste.

5. Add the tinned tomatoes, beef stock and light soy sauce. Leave to simmer for a minimum of 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

### Béchamel

6. Place a saucepan on medium heat and add the black sesame seeds. Toast for a minute or two.

7. Add the butter. Once melted, sprinkle in the flour and whisk until a paste forms.

8. Add the milk gradually while whisking, until you have a smooth sauce.

9. Season with salt and stir in the grated cheddar cheese.

### Assembly

10. Preheat the oven to 200°C.

11. Spoon a thin layer of ragu onto the base of the dish, then layer with lasagna sheets, ragu, béchamel. Repeat this process 6 times. The top layer should be béchamel sauce.

12. Top with torn pieces of mozzarella and grated parmesan.

13. Bake for 40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
